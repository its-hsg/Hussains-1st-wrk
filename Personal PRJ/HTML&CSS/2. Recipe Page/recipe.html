Pasta Delight

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Garlic, onions, and your favorite veggies

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic and onions, add veggies.
  3. Mix in tomato sauce, toss with pasta.

Meaty Marvel

Ingredients:

  • 250g meaty pasta
  • 1 cup hearty meat sauce
  • Garlic, onions, and your favorite meat-friendly veggies

Instructions:

  1. Cook meaty pasta until it's got that perfect bite.
  2. Sauté garlic and onions, add your favorite meaty delights.
  3. Mix in the hearty meat sauce, toss it all together.

Jollof Joy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of parboiled rice
  • 1 cup of tomato stew
  • Pepper, onions, and your favorite veggie squad
  • Afrobeat playlist for those good vibes

Instructions:

  1. Parboil the rice - make it dance in hot water until it's almost there.
  2. Sauté pepper and onions, let the kitchen smell like a celebration.
  3. Introduce the tomato stew, let it mingle with the party.
  4. Add the rice, let it absorb all that flavorful joy.
